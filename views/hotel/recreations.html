About the Dance Show

The Aphrodite Cabaret Show makes for a fun night out. The cabaret show is housed in a ship-like structure and in addition to a huge theatre, there are restaurants and a brewery for visitors to enjoy a few drinks and some food before the show. Prepare to be entertained during one hours with many dances and musical numbers. There is a range of options for all travellers such as Wifi equipped amenities, gym, fitness centres, and complimentary breakfast.
